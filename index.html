Frequently asked

How long does a remodel take?

Typical garage remodels run 1–2 weeks depending on scope. New builds vary by permitting and complexity.

Do you offer design‑only services?

Yes. We can produce layouts, finish schedules, and equipment specs with vendor lists.

Which smart platforms do you support?

Apple Home, Google Home, Alexa, and platform‑agnostic hubs like Home Assistant.
